Our Secret to the
Perfect BBQ Dishes

At The Mills BBQ, our secret lies in our perfectly blended and delicious homemade sauces. We've always believed that grilled and smoked meats taste their best when enhanced with an irresistible combination of sweet, sour, salty, or spicy flavours.

Boasting hot, traditional, Texan BBQ sauces, along with 100% pure maple syrup, we have everything we need to satisfy the most demanding palates. With the finest ingredients, we carefully marinate all our meats for 3 days, including our halal beef ribs and turkey sausages. Next we cook your meal slowly for 16 hours on wood charcoal with our state-of-the-art hickory smokers.

To complete your meal, we also offer delectable side dishes like cornbread muffins, baked potatoes and our famous Holy Balls (fried dumplings). Stop by our BBQ food truck and expect nothing less than the tastiest food in town. Place your order today!
